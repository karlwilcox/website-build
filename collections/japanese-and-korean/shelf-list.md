I blame the attractive cover art making me buy too many books, but I'm currently only about half way through reading
these. You'll find reviews of those I have actually read
by searching for the tag ["japan"](/tags/japan/) so at this point I'll just
list them by title.

* Before the Coffee Gets Cold - Toshikazu Kawaguchi
* Before Your Memory Fades - Toshikazu Kawaguchi
* Tales From the Cafe - Toshikazu Kawaguchi
* Before We Say Goodbye - Toshikazu Kawaguchi
* Before We Forget Kindness - Toshikazu Kawaguchi
* Welcome to the Hyunam-Dong Bookshop - Hwang Bo-reum
* The Door-to-Door Bookstore - Garsten Henn
* Butter - Asako Yuzuki
* The Cat and the City - Nick Bradley (a British author trying his own take on the genre!)
* The Kamagowa Food Detectives - Hisashi Kashiwai
* The Restaurant of Lost Recipes - Hisashi Kashiwai
* The Dallergut Dream Department Store - Miye Lee
* Return to the Dallergut Dream Department Store - Miye Lee
* Norwegian Wood - Haruki Murakami
* Scattered All Over The Earth - Yoko Tawada
* Sympathy Tower Tokyo - Rie Qudan
* Days at the Morisaki Bookshop - Satoshi Yagisawa
* More Days at the Morisaki Bookshop - Satoshi Yagisawa
* Days at the Torunka Cafe - Satoshi Yagisawa
* Murder at the Black Cat Cafe - Seishi Yokomizo
* Strange Pictures - Uketsu
* Strange Houses - Uketsu
* What You Are Looking For is in the Library - Michiko Aoyama
* Beautiful Star - Yukio Mishima
* The Ark Sakura - Kobo Abe
* The Full Moon Coffee Shop - Mai Mochizuki
* The Vanishing Cherry Blossom Bookshop - Takya Asakura
* The Bookshop Woman - Nanako Hanada (not a novel, but reads like one!)
* Strange Weather in Tokyo - Hiromi Kawakami
* The Nakano Thrift Shop - Hiromi Kawakami
* All the Lovers in the Night- Mieko Kawakami
* The Convenience Store by the Sea - Sonoko Machida

(I also have some works by Kazuo Ishiguro and Haruki Murakami that
aren't set *in* Japan, so I'm not including them here.)
