The main books that I have collected so far are listed below, along with the periods covered.

* To Hell and Back (1914 - 1949) - Ian Kershaw (A slightly wider European perspective)
* Roller-coaster (1950 - 2017) - Ian Kershaw (ditto)
* Britain's War: Into Battle (1937 - 1941) - Daniel Todman
* Britain's War: A New World (1942 - 1947) - Daniel Todman
* Austerity Britain (1945 - 1951) - David Kynaston
* Family Britain (1951 - 1957) - David Kynaston
* Modernity Britain (1957 - 1962) - David Kynaston
* A Northern Wind (1962 - 1965) - David Kynaston
* Never Had it So Good (1956 - 1963) - Dominic Sandbrook
* Whte Heat (1964 - 1970) - Dominic Sandbrook
* State of Emergency (1970 - 1974) - Dominic Sandbrook
* Seasons in the Sun (1974 - 1979) - Dominic Sandbrook
* Who Dares Wins (1979 - 1982) - Dominic Sandbrook
* Haywire (2000 - 2025) Andrew Hindmoor

Obviously there's a bit of gap in the 80's and 90's - if you have
suggestions to fill it please let me know in the comments below!
