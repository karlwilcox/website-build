Manga Backgrounds

I like the simple accuracy of Manga, clean lines with realistic perspective; especially scenic backgrounds. Most these started life as pencil and ink outlines on cartridge paper, which were then scanned and coloured in Smith Micro's Clip Studio Pro (previously Manga Studio).
